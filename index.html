about liberia safe space initiative

The Liberia Safe Space initiative (LISS Initiative) is a non-governmental organization dedicated to raising awareness, providing education, amd advocating for mental health policy reforms in Liberia,especially for the youth. With a focus on rebuilding mental health infrastructure and addressing the deep psychology impact of Liberia's civil war, the Ebola crisis, and the increased in narcotics drugs and substance used amongst the youth population, we are working to ensure that mental health services become accessible and stigma-free across Liberia.

Our goal is to create a safe space where mental health is prioritized, and every young person and community member affected by trauma or mental challenges has access to the support they need to thrive emotionally and psychologically.
